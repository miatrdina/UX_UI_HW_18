USER EMPATHY

User empathy is the ability to understand and relate to the feelings, motivations, and experiences of users, which helps in creating more user-centered designs and solutions. While there are no specific tools for empathy, techniques like user interviews, contextual inquiries, and persona development can foster empathy by deepening understanding of users' perspectives and needs.

BUSINESS ACCUMEN
